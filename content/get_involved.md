## Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with the Youngstown Action Center!

We are always looking for individuals, organizations, and projects that align with our mission and values. You and/or your organization does not need to be affiliated with the YAC to work with our organizers or use our space. If you'd like to discuss an idea, project, or event that you believe would help us enrich our community, [contact the YAC today!](mailto:youngstownactioncenter@proton.me)

If you are interested in joining or contacting any of our affiliated organizations, each organization's contact information and social media links may be found in [our Org Directory.](@/org_directory.md)
