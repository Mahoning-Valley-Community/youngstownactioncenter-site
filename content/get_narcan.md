## NARCAN Distribution

The Youngstown Action Center is a NARCAN distribution site. NARCAN, or naxolone, may be used to prevent death in the case of opioid overdose. If you or anyone you know is in need of NARCAN, [contact the YAC today.](mailto:youngstownactioncenter@proton.me)
